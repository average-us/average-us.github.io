### Ideas and Other Stuff

I've been messing around a little bit with the Spotify API stuff using the Spotipy library, and it's pretty cool. You can do stuff like query for information about songs, make playlists, and even see what you're listening to.


Basically what I'll be trying to do (my idea) is that I'll listen to music for a little a while and log which songs I skip (do not enjoy) and which ones I don't skip (enjoy). I'll log the song, its features, the time it was listened to, and whether it was skipped or not, and use that data to train a model to recommend me a song that a won't skip based of what I'm currently listening to.


I'm still working on it though.
