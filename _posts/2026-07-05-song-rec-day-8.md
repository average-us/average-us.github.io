### Update

The lack of acutal audio features is really hurting, as the model is only trying to recommend things off of very surface level features like artist name, whether it's explicit or not, things like that. It can't learn what the songs are actually like, and therefore generalize to songs it's never heard before.

I'll still keep trying to make things work. I might even try to shift to something like an open source dataset due to the limitations the Spotfiy API has.
