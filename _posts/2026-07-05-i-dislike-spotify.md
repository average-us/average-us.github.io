So Spotify deprecated a bunch of the endpoints like audio features, related artists, artist genre, etc. As I mentioned before, this is a pretty major blow to my project, but we keep on moving.


Anyways, I thought I found a workaround, in a third party API called SoundNet, but apparently, the free plan has 5 calls per day, in a hard limit. Are you kidding me? In my 600 song playlist that I'm trying to recommend for, at this rate, it would take a literal year to gather all that data, when it would have taken minutes before Spotify removed those features.
