### The New Stuff

I'm still using the GTZAN dataset, but this time, I'm extracting audio features from each file, and using that to create a similarity matrix, utilizing cosine similarity. Each song is being represented as a higher dimensional feature vector, and the dot product is being used to see how similar each song is to each other.

What the cosine similaity (normalized dot product) does is see how "parallel" two vectors are. If they are parallel (the similarity score is 1.0), then the two songs (vectors) are identical. If they are perpendicular (similarity score of 0.0), then the two songs are wildy different.
